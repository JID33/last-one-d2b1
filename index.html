Investment Program Eligibility Quiz

This quiz is designed to assess if your preferences align with the philosophy of our investment program, which emphasizes sharing and collaboration, mirroring the calculations of our simulator.

This is not a professional financial assessment, but a tool to help you think about what is most important to you in a sharing-focused investment program.

Instructions:

Please answer each of the following questions. There are no right or wrong answers.

1. What is your main goal when investing?
2. How do you manage program membership and participation fees?
3. What is your view on members' contribution to the program's success?
4. In case of a personal financial unforeseen event or for another member, what is your expectation from the group?
5. What is most important to you in managing an investment program?
